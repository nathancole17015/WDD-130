People will come to my site to see work that I have completed. This meant for future employers and recruiters. I want a place to display work that I have done to show off my skills. I hope that the future employers would appreciate a place that they can go to see a collection of programs I have made. Visitors will come to my site over others because it will display information on me. This site is totally just to display information on me. It will also have contact information if anyone would have questions, I will display my email address. On the home page it has a picture of me and the mission for the website.
